ONLY FAST. After every 8 yards Chou moves, his next Basic Attack will deal 200% of damage (can't be critical) and shortly slow enemies by 80%.
JEET KUNE DO. Chou punches forward for 3 times continously. Each punch deals 180 (+70% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage. The 3rd attack knocks nearby enemies into the air. The 3rd use of this skill resets the CD of Shunpo.
SHUNPO. Chou dashes forward, becoming immune to Control Effects during the dash. After the dash, Chou increases his Physical Pentration by 15 points and obtains a Shield that can absorb 200 (+150% Total Physical ATK) points of damage for 2s.
THE WAY OF DRAGON. Chou casts a roundhouse kick to knock back enemies and deals 400 (+200% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage. Use Again: Chou will chase up the enemies in the air, dealing at least 400 (+200% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage.
